## Hi, I’m **James**  (or **jamcre**)

I’m a Security+ certified Computer Scientist with a focus on cybersecurity, endpoint hardening, and secure infrastructure. I built this site as a place to document my homelab, share what I’m learning, and give people a look into how I approach security, systems, and continuous improvement.

## What I Work On

My projects span across enterprise security, homelab, and embedded systems:

- Hardening Windows endpoints with enterprise tooling like CrowdStrike, Darktrace, and Halcyon  
- Designing segmented network architectures using OpenWRT, VLANs, and Proxmox  
- Deploying Wazuh SIEM and analyzing real‑time telemetry across multi‑OS environments  
- Running cyber ranges, performing enumeration, and validating exploitation techniques  
- Building secure IoT pipelines with ESP32, TLS‑MQTT, and custom GUIs  

Whether it’s a hypervisor cluster, a Docker stack, or an embedded sensor network, I like building systems that are secure, observable, and resilient.
